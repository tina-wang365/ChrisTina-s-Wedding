About Christopher and Tina

Christopher and Tina have known each other as college friends at the University of California of Riverside for about four years. They were both active members of their computer science and engineering club on campus and sought to help their fellow peers through tutoring to forge friendships, network opportunities and promote computer science. They played Kirby's Epic Yarn at school almost every Friday. Christopher proposed to Tina on Saturday, March 22nd. With common interests and beliefs, they became best friends and started an official relationship on April 25th, 2014.
